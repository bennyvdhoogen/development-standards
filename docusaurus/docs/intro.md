## Format of standard

Standards are described using the following headings:

- Title of standard 

- When due another review 

- Why this standard 

- When and for whom is this standard?  

- How to apply this standard? 

- What pitfalls to avoid?* 

- Considerations 

- Further reading 

- Acknowledgments
